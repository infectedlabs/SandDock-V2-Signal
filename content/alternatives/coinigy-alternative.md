## What is Coinigy and who is it designed for?

Coinigy is a multi-exchange trading terminal designed for active cryptocurrency traders who want a single, consolidated interface to monitor markets, conduct technical analysis, and execute trades.

The platform's main feature is its integration capability. By connecting exchange API keys, users can track balances and place orders across dozens of exchanges (like Binance, Bitfinex, Coinbase, and Kraken) without logging into each exchange individually. It uses TradingView charting libraries, providing traders with an extensive set of technical indicators, drawing tools, and historical data.

It is a specialized tool for manual traders who spend hours sitting in front of multiple monitors, managing portfolios, and drawing support and resistance lines.

## Why do traders look for a Coinigy alternative?

Traders search for Coinigy alternatives because manual charting is highly time-consuming and mentally exhausting. Additionally, managing API keys for multiple exchanges poses security risks, and Coinigy lacks built-in signal alerts or analysis tools.

The primary challenges with Coinigy include:
- **Time Commitment**: To trade profitably using manual charting, you must constantly monitor price action across multiple timeframes. This is difficult for casual traders who have full-time jobs or other commitments.
- **API Management and Security**: Giving a third-party platform write and trade permissions for all your exchange accounts creates a single point of failure. If your Coinigy account is compromised, your connected exchange funds are at risk.
- **Lack of Guidance**: Coinigy does not offer insights, AI explanations, or trade ideas. If you make a mistake in your technical analysis, the platform will not warn you.

These limitations have led many retail investors to look for signal-based tools that require no API connections.
