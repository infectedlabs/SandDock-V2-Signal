## What is CryptoSignals.org and what do they promise?

CryptoSignals.org is an online subscription service that sends daily trading alerts for major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Cardano, and Polkadot. The alerts are distributed to paying members via a private Telegram channel.

The platform promises to deliver signals developed by a team of professional traders with years of market experience. They advertise a win rate of up to 82%, offering a mix of short-term day trades and medium-term swing trades. Each signal typically contains the target cryptocurrency, the entry price zone, a stop-loss limit, and three take-profit targets. Subscribers are encouraged to set up their trades on a recommended broker or exchange and let the signals play out.

While the packaging of these signals is straightforward, the primary concerns lie in the discrepancy between their marketing claims and the actual experiences of paying subscribers.

## What are the Trustpilot complaints saying about CryptoSignals.org?

Numerous public reviews on Trustpilot label the service a scam, primarily citing a massive gap between advertised success and actual performance. Users report that the group often deletes or ignores losing trades in their summary reports.

On Trustpilot, CryptoSignals.org has faced a significant volume of highly critical feedback. The recurring themes in these complaints include:
- **Discrepancy in Performance**: Users complain that the signals they receive in the VIP Telegram channel lead to consistent drawdowns, which contrasts sharply with the high win rates claimed on the website.
- **Selective Results Reporting**: Reviewers note that when a signal hitting stop-loss is generated, the channel administrators frequently delete the message or simply omit it from their weekly and monthly summary reports, leaving only the winning signals visible to prospective buyers.
- **Aggressive Upselling**: Several users report being pressured by support reps to upgrade to more expensive lifetime VIP plans or sign up with specific partner brokers to unlock "guaranteed" profits.

This pattern of behavior has caused many community members to advise caution when dealing with the platform.

## Why is getting a refund so difficult with CryptoSignals.org?

Getting a refund is notoriously difficult due to strict cancellation clauses, automatic billing renewals without notice, and a support team that frequently ignores cancellation requests. Many users must file chargebacks to stop payments.

The refund and billing issues surrounding CryptoSignals.org are a primary source of user frustration. The main friction points include:
1. **Unnotified Auto-Renewals**: Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of the billing period without sending a reminder email, catching users off guard.
2. **Delayed Support Responses**: Users trying to cancel their subscriptions before the renewal date report that the customer service team takes days to respond, often letting the billing cycle roll over before taking action.
3. **Rigid Terms of Service**: The platform's refund policy contains numerous clauses that make obtaining a refund virtually impossible once a payment has processed, even if the service was not used.

As a result, many users report having to contact their banks or payment processors directly to file dispute claims or block future charges.
